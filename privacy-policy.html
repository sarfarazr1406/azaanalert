Azaan Alert

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
Azaan Alert is designed to work entirely on your device. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your location is used only to calculate prayer times and is never sent to any server.

📍 Data We Access

Location

Azaan Alert requests access to your device's GPS coordinates solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your current position. Your coordinates are also used to determine your country so the app can automatically select the regionally appropriate prayer time calculation method.

Location is processed entirely on-device using the Android Geocoder and the Adhan library. It is never transmitted to any server, never stored in the cloud, and never shared with any third party.

Preferences & Settings

Your preferences — including calculation method, madhab selection, per-prayer enable/disable toggles, and notification settings — are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device.

🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information of any kind
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data
  • Account credentials — no registration is required
  • Contacts, messages, camera, or microphone data
  • Any data unrelated to prayer time calculation

🔐 Permissions Explained

Permission Why it is needed
Location (Fine & Coarse) Calculate prayer times and detect your region for the correct calculation method
Post Notifications Display the Azan alert at prayer time
Schedule Exact Alarm Fire the Azan notification at the precise prayer time
Foreground Service Play the Azan audio without interruption
Receive Boot Completed Restore scheduled alarms after a device restart
Use Full Screen Intent Display the prayer alert on the lock screen when the alarm fires

No permission is used for any purpose beyond what is listed above.

🔊 Audio

The Azan audio played by this app is bundled within the app itself. No audio is streamed from the internet. The app does not record or access your microphone at any time.
