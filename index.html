What's inside
Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Azaan Alert is lightweight, private, and works completely offline.

📍

On-Device Calculation

Prayer times are calculated locally using your GPS coordinates and the Adhan library. No internet connection required.

🌍

Auto Region Detection

Your country is detected from your location and used to automatically select the correct regional calculation method.

🔔

Exact Alarm Scheduling

Alarms fire at the precise prayer time using Android's exact alarm API, even in low-power mode.

🔊

Beautiful Azan Audio

A high-quality bundled Azan plays as a foreground service. Pause or resume directly from the notification.

🔒

Lock Screen Support

When an alarm fires, the app appears over your lock screen so you never miss the prayer alert.

⚙️

Per-Prayer Controls

Enable or disable each of the 5 daily prayers individually. Switch to notification-only mode if you prefer silence.

🔄

Auto-Refresh

Prayer times update automatically in the background when you travel, so you're always on local time.

🌙

Light & Dark Theme

A carefully crafted theme follows your system preference, built around a distinctive lime-green palette.
